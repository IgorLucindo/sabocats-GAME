ART:
    map:
    - lobby: 2D side-view platformer game level background, pixel art style. A goofy, cartoonish city alleyway in a vibrant criminal underworld. Designed as a continuous left-to-right progression: the far left edge opens to a main city street, transitioning rightward into an enclosed alley, and terminating completely at a solid dead-end wall on the far right edge. Orthographic projection, zero-point perspective, perfectly flat camera angle with NO vanishing point and NO depth. The entire back wall is the flat brick side of a building, perfectly parallel to the screen. The environment is scaled for a tiny cat protagonist; trash cans and lower windows are sized for a small feline. Exaggerated chunky shapes, bold black outlines, vibrant and slightly saturated colors, no pastels, no dark muddy colors. Inspired by the art style of 'Nobody Saves the World'. Pure side-scrolling game asset.

    character:
    - player: 2D side-view platformer game character sprite, pixel art style. A tiny, shy, street cat in a goofy, cartoonish vibrant criminal underworld. The cat has a 'poor' look: scruffy, slightly messy fur, a slouching hesitant posture. Style requirements: Exaggerated chunky shapes, bold thick black outlines, vibrant and slightly saturated colors, no pastels, no dark muddy colors. Inspired by the art style of 'Nobody Saves the World'. Pure side-scrolling character asset, neutral idle pose, orthographic projection.
